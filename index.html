Who are we?

We are a group of young people currently taking part in the National Citizen Service scheme. Music for Mind is our social action project.

What are our aims?

We aim to help raise awareness for young peoples mental health as well as raise money for Mind, a UK based mental health charity.

Where will the money go?

All funds raised as part of this social action project will go to Mind, a charity registered in England (no. 219830).
