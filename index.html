Who are we?

We are a group of young people currently taking part in the National Citizen Service scheme. Feeding Our Future is our social action project.

What are our aims?

We aim to raise awareness about food poverty in the UK, particularly in the North West. We have chosen to raise awareness about the Olive Branch, a local charity that helps those in need with food parcles as well as someone to talk to.
